    About Us

    SUPERFLEX LOGISTIC INC. aims to lead the 21st century as a global packaging company and is committed to the timely delivery of quality products to its customers thereby giving all customers total satisfaction by continuously improving its integrated management systems with due care to environment and health and safety of its employeees and stakeholders.

    Our Mission

    To supply our customers with high quality packaging products complemented with competitive prices and exceptional just-in-time deliveries.

    To provide our employees a good working environment that will enhance professional growth and competence to enable them to be the best in the industry and to very responsive to the needs of our customers

    Our Objective

    To serve as a guide in achieving the management's goal of effective controlling the operations, eliminating non-conformance at all stages, and promoting a culture of "prevention" so that the problems are anticipated and intercepted bofore they develope.

    To Provide all employees a complete description of the Quality Management System and also serve as reference for the orientation of newly hired employees.

    Our Vision

    To be a leader in packaging and logistic industry with established world class resource management system providing excelent products, services and high return value to customer.

    Services

    Packaging and Products

    SFLI designs, manufactures and sells a wide range of packaging and cushioning products for various industry requirements. These are made out from corrugated sheets, regenerated paper, pulp and foamed plastic (polyethylene, polypropylene and polystyrene foam). We have a strong design and implementation capability.


    We at SFLI are committed in establishing this type of working partnership with our clients in order to meet customer demands, in terms of costs and quality. We complement this by setting up the most appropriate and efficient delivery scheme for their business. This capability is built on our considerable experience in the packaging business catering to retail products, electronics and automotive industries. The use of modem information technology is incorporated in all SFLI business and internal transactions to allow the efficient monitoring and supervision of production requirements as well as delivery schedules.

    Logistics and Warehousing

    SFLI will endeover to provide the product and service requirement of clients so that, they in return, concentrate in their own core business. In order to achieve this, the management team of SFLI has streamlined its operation and administrative structure to cater the dynamic needs of the customers. Every SFLI personnel is mindful of the company's motto of providing goods and services "Beyond Logistics".


    Super Flex Logistic has 1000 sq. m. available space for your warehousing need. We offer long term storage, giving our customers the ability to pull from their inventory and make use of our many services. Whether you need to store for a couple of months or for a couple of years, we have a solution to your storage and inventory needs.
